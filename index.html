About Us

What you need to know

Who We Are

Supermarket Services Company Inc. sells and installs walk-in or reach-in coolers and freezers from Crown Tonka, US Cooler, Carroll Coolers, Turbo Air, True, and MAXX COLD. We focus on the grocery industry, commercial refrigerated warehouses, and convenience store, drug store, and package store industries.

What We Believe

It is our primary goal to ensure that you receive the highest quality equipment, tailored specifically to your particular needs and requirements. From store design to construction planning to the final completed project Supermarket Services Company is your one stop shop.
